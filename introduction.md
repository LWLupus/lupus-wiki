# Introduction

## Browser

You have to choose a good browser. Choosing one of the browsers below and not choosing a browser like chrome is REALLY essential.

* [🌟**Brave**](https://brave.com/) - You get all the benefits of chromium without all the tracking.

::: details
Brave has **Ad-Blocking** built in.     

It also has an ai assistant that is *good enough*. It also has something called **Rewards / BAT** it awards you a crypto called **BAT** when you see their adds.**(It is optional and turned off by default)** Their adds are not intrusive and just appear on the homescreen. They also have a built in secure wallet to store your crypto. They also have **Brave talk** that is like zoom but free and no login. And they also have brave **NEWS** that is private.

Most of theese features are sites made by the brave company and not built into the browser.
:::

* [**FireFox**](https://www.firefox.com/) - Firefox is a privacy focused open source browser.


## Ad Blocker

With brave you dont **need** an ad blocker but it is still nice to have.

* [🌟**UBO**](https://ublockorigin.com/)/[Brave](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/ublock-origin/cjpalhdlnbpafiamejdnhcphjbkeiagm)/[Firefox](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/ublock-origin/) - U Block Origin extention ad blocker is the best adblocker you can get.

* [**UBDNS**](https://ublockdns.com/) - U Block DNS is the same thing as the extention but on a higher level. The extention can only block stuff on your browser while the DNS block stuff ALL over your device.

::: warning
UBDNS is more unstable and not recomended to beginners.
:::
